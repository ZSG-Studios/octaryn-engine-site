Version 1.0

This Contributor Assignment Agreement ("Agreement") is between Zachary David
Rose ("Maintainer") and the contributor accepting this Agreement
("Contributor").

Effective date: the date on which Contributor accepts this Agreement through the
official Octaryn Engine contributor intake form or another written acceptance
method designated by the Maintainer.

## 1. Purpose

Octaryn Engine is an access-controlled, source-available, proprietary project. The
Maintainer will only review or accept substantive contributions from
contributors who agree through the repository's contributor intake process or
another designated written acceptance method that ownership and use rights in
those contributions are handled under this Agreement.

## 2. Definitions

"Project" means Octaryn Engine.

"Contribution" means any code, patch, pull request, commit, issue attachment,
script, asset, documentation, design file, test, shader, tool, or other material
submitted by Contributor for possible inclusion in the Project.
